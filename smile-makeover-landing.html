Cosmetic Dentistry Services

Customized solutions for your perfect smile

Porcelain Veneers

Ultra-thin shells of medical-grade ceramic bonded to the front of your teeth. Perfect for fixing chips, stains, gaps, and misalignment. The ultimate solution for a Hollywood smile.

  • Natural appearance: Translucent like real enamel
  • Stain resistant: Keep your bright smile for years
  • Minimal prep: Preserve your natural tooth structure
  • Custom designed: Perfectly matched to your face and features
Ideal For:
Chipped, stained, gapped, or slightly crooked teeth

Dental Crowns

Custom-crafted caps that cover the entire visible portion of your tooth. Restore strength, function, and beauty to damaged or weakened teeth. Built to last with porcelain or zirconia.

  • Full protection: Covers and strengthens entire tooth
  • Durable materials: Withstands normal biting forces
  • Natural look: Colored and shaped to match your smile
  • Long lifespan: 10-15+ years with proper care
Ideal For:
Cracked, broken, heavily filled, or root canal treated teeth

Complete Smile Makeovers

Comprehensive transformation combining multiple procedures—veneers, crowns, whitening, bonding, and more. Completely redesign your smile to achieve your dream aesthetic and function.

  • Custom treatment plan: Tailored to your goals and budget
  • Digital smile preview: See results before we start
  • Coordinated care: All treatments work together harmoniously
  • Life-changing results: Total smile transformation
Ideal For:
Multiple cosmetic concerns, total smile transformation

Full Mouth Reconstruction

Comprehensive restoration addressing severe decay, damage, or wear. Combines restorative and cosmetic treatments to rebuild your entire bite, function, and appearance from the ground up.

  • Functional restoration: Correct bite, jaw alignment, chewing
  • Multiple disciplines: Crowns, implants, bridges, and more
  • Pain relief: Eliminate chronic discomfort and TMJ issues
  • Total transformation: New smile, new confidence, new life
Ideal For:
Extensive damage, severe wear, failing dental work, functional problems

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a smile makeover take?
It depends on your treatment plan. Simple cases with veneers can be completed in 2-3 visits over 2-4 weeks. Full mouth reconstructions may take several months. We'll give you a clear timeline during your consultation.
Will veneers or crowns look natural?
Absolutely. Modern porcelain materials are incredibly lifelike. We custom-design each restoration to match your facial features, skin tone, and personality. No one will know unless you tell them—they'll just think you have a beautiful natural smile.
Are veneers and crowns permanent?
They're considered permanent because we prepare the tooth structure, but the restorations themselves may eventually need replacement. Veneers typically last 10-20 years, crowns 10-15+ years. With excellent care, many patients never need replacements.
Does cosmetic dentistry hurt?
No. We use local anesthesia to keep you comfortable during any prep work. Most patients report minimal to no discomfort. We also offer sedation options if you're anxious. After treatment, any sensitivity typically resolves within a few days.
Can I see what my new smile will look like beforehand?
Yes! We use digital smile design technology to create a preview of your new smile. You'll see the results before we even begin treatment, and we can make adjustments until you're thrilled with the design.
Does insurance cover cosmetic dentistry?
It depends. Purely cosmetic work usually isn't covered, but restorative procedures like crowns for damaged teeth often are. We'll verify your benefits and maximize your insurance coverage. Even without insurance, we work with 3rd party lenders to provide financing options to help make treatment affordable.
