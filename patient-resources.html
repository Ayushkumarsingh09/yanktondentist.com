Patient Resources

Everything you need to prepare for your appointment and make the most of your visit.

New Patients

Welcome to our dental family!

What to Bring

  • Valid photo ID (driver's license or state ID)
  • Insurance cards (dental and medical)
  • List of current medications and dosages
  • Previous dental records or X-rays (if available)
  • Payment method (credit card, debit, cash, or check)

What to Expect

  • Paperwork: We'll ask you to complete health history forms
  • Exam: Comprehensive oral health evaluation
  • X-Rays: Digital X-rays if needed (safe and fast)
  • Cleaning: Professional cleaning if time allows
  • Treatment Plan: Discussion of findings and recommendations

Office Policies

Appointments

We schedule appointments Monday through Friday. Same-day emergency appointments are often available. Please call or text us to schedule your visit.

Cancellations

We understand that schedules change. Please provide at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. This allows us to offer your time slot to another patient in need.

Running Late

If you're running late, please let us know as soon as possible. We'll do our best to accommodate you, though we may need to reschedule depending on how delayed you are.

Emergencies

Dental emergencies happen. Call us immediately if you experience severe pain, trauma, swelling, or other urgent dental issues. We'll make every effort to see you the same day.

Privacy & HIPAA

Your privacy is important to us. We comply with all HIPAA regulations to protect your personal health information. We will never share your information without your written consent, except as required by law.
