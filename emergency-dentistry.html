What To Do in a Dental Emergency

Quick action can make all the difference. Here's what to do while you're on your way to see us.

01

Knocked-Out Tooth

  • • Handle by the crown (top), not the root
  • • Rinse gently if dirty—don't scrub
  • • Try to reinsert in socket, or keep in milk
  • • Call us immediately—time is critical
02

Severe Toothache

  • • Rinse mouth with warm salt water
  • • Floss gently to remove trapped debris
  • • Apply cold compress to reduce swelling
  • • Take over-the-counter pain relief
03

Broken Tooth

  • • Save any broken pieces
  • • Rinse mouth with warm water
  • • Apply gauze to stop any bleeding
  • • Use cold compress for pain and swelling

The most important step? Call us right away.
